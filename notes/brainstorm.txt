Modes:
  Planning - build a workout plan
  Active - execute a workout, often according to preexisting workout plan
  Record - record a workout
  Review - observe progress through lists and graphs

Planning Actions
  Add Exercise
  Make workout (exercises, RPE, goal weight/reps for each exercise)
  Make Mesocycle
  Make Macrocycle
  

Active Actions
  Start workout
    Specify the associated planned workout
  Record sets & weight for each workout
